Feature One

Music

Piano, production, and composition for film and game.

Piano came first. Classical training from age 4 till 16, completing ABRSM. Composition, Arrangement, and Improvisation followed.

Through-composed solo piano, cues against picture lock, full scores for video games, and client mixing and mastering. Twenty-plus tracks live across the major streaming platforms; tens of original piano pieces are still quietly accumulating on the hard drive, waiting for their turn.

The recognition I'm most quietly proud of is Best Audio at Game Zanga, MENA-wide, 2025. Performing with the National Music Conservatory Orchestra on French Horn, including a concert for His Majesty the King of Jordan.
